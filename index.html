Sandpoint Auto Detail LLC

At Sandpoint Auto Detail, you have the choice of bringing your car to our shop or letting us come directly to your location, providing both interior and exterior cleaning, along with engine bay detailing for a complete car refresh.

Our wide range of experience and collaborative approach has afforded us an extensive network of long-lasting relationships. Clearshield Wrap

Make your car's true color shine for as long as possible with Paint Protective Film. PPF is the best way to protect your paint against rock chips and scratches. This clear shield wrap can help keep your car's surface looking flawless. It's practically invisible and it has self-healing properties which means minor scratches and swirl marks vanish when exposed to heat or sunlight.

Window Tint

Having tinted windows reduces glare, adds privacy and drastically extends the life of your interior. It can protect your upholstery from discoloration and keep it cool by blocking damaging UV light.
